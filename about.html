APT Geotechnical Solutions Ltd (AGSL) is a specialized, Zambian consulting and contracting engineering company, that was incorporated in 2018 after the realization by two engineers that their practical professional experience which spans over 35 years combined, could be useful in developing a sustainable engineering consultancy business.

AGSL's formation was on the basis of extensive geotechnical, geo-hydrological and geological consultancy that had been done in Zambia, Malawi, Tanzania and The Democratic Republic of Congo.
